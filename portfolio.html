About Me

A designer who
thinks in stories.

Hello, I'm Najat Ibrahim — a 3rd year BSIT student at Davao del Norte State College with a deep love for graphic design and user experience. I believe purposeful design is the most elegant form of communication.

My work spans brand identity, poster design, promotional materials, and UI design. I served as the UI/UX Designer for SocioQuest, an AI-driven cybersecurity training platform built with my team, Pink Hat Hackers.

I design primarily in Figma and Canva, and build with VS Code. I'm always exploring the intersection where aesthetics and function become one.
